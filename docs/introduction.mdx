# Welcome to ZebraTruth AI

ZebraTruth AI provides **Compliance Intelligence as a Service**. You keep your video editors, content pipelines, workflows and creative tools. ZebraTruth's AI agents are embedded into your content creation workflows and return structured compliance results with **annotations that map directly to your UI**. 

Instant compliance checks are proactive and take place within your creation, upstream of production, instead of passive post-production checks. Full audit report to help you make your AI content deliverable and legally defensible.

## How It Works

1. **Send content** — POST your ad script, images, or video metadata to the API
2. **Agents analyze** — 20\+ specialized AI agents check against laws, platforms, and rights
3. **Get results** — Structured report with score, checks, and UI-embeddable annotations
4. **Fix and re-check** — Iterate until the decision is PUBLISH
